My recent project with ROSIE Lab!

My project is called "Aware Like an Animal."

The goal is to create an interactive stimuli dataset for programmers and designers to use towards making believable, interaction-aware virtual characters.

As the undergraduate research assistant, I analyzed and annotated participants' videos. I helped to edit and proofread the paper, and participated and collaborated in meetings. I also created a supplementary video overviewing our project to submit to CHI '24 and UbiComp '24.
